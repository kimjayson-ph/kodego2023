About

My name is Brian and I want to get real with you for a minute. I’ve always dreamed big, wanted to make something of my life, and deeply cared about people.

I could list a bunch of things that would make me look “important” and “successful”, but what I want you to know about me is I truly care about people, our planet, and about maximizing my potential. I’m committed to knowing that when I leave this world that my tank was empty, because I gave ALL that I had and then some in the name of LOVE.

So whether I’m speaking, leading workshops, writing books, or making conscious content through social media, it’s all in SERVICE of the same thing….LOVE. It’s the answer to everything and it’s so obvious that some of us miss it. So I’m here as a messenger to remind us all that when we AMPLIFY our LOVE we automatically amplify our LIVES.

Wedding

Officiating a wedding and helping you prepare for marriage is a free service we provide to our Victory members. It is our joy to assist you in this exciting season of your life.

PLEASE TAKE NOTE:

Officiating a wedding is a service we offer to our members in Victory Fort. We believe that this is a special event in one’s life that needs ample preparation so we encourage you to get in touch with us at least 3 months before your wedding date. This will give you enough time to experience our Blueprint for Marriage Pre-Marriage coaching and secure all required documents. After filling up your online form , kindly wait for the Pastoral Care Administrator to contact you for a wedding interview schedule.

Baby Dedication

Dedicating your child recognizes God’s faithfulness, and sovereignty over the life of the parents and child. We minister to parents as they dedicate themselves in raising and entrusting the life of the child to God’s will.

PLEASE TAKE NOTE:

BABY DEDICATION is a service we offer to our members in Victory Fort. We believe this is a milestone in your life as parents. Please note that Baby Dedication will be in Group setting at Every Nation Building happening once a month, starting March 2023. We highly encourage you to apply at least one (1) month prior the ceremony. This will give you enough time to experience our Heart of Parenting lessons, and meet with a parenting coach for two (2) meetings. After filling up your online form , kindly wait for the Pastoral Care Administrator to contact you for a baby-dedication interview schedule.
